Welcome

Just as Robinhood has made investing more accessible through its commission-free mobile and web applications, so too does CoinLogic desire to provide an interface that makes valuable investment knowledge available in a beautiful and easily approachable way.

CoinLogic provides the user with a personalized dashboard to access a selected firm’s 13F filing. Unlike the publicly accessible data on the SEC’s website, CoinLogic displays the information in a pleasant and manipulable manner.

Since many regular investors have never heard of or have never worked with 13F filings, our initial goal is to provide the following informational page to inform our users.
